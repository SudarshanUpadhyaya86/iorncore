Got Questions?

FREQUENTLY ASKED

Can I switch plans after signing up? ▼
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your dashboard. Changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.
Is there a joining fee or hidden charges? ▼
Zero joining fee, zero hidden charges. You pay only the monthly plan price shown. No surprises, ever.
What if I want to cancel my membership? ▼
Cancel anytime from your dashboard with one click. We'll keep your access active until the end of your paid period. No questions asked.
Do the personal trainer sessions roll over? ▼
Pro plan sessions are valid for 30 days and do not roll over. We encourage you to use them all — that's where the real results happen!
Can I bring a friend with the Basic plan? ▼
The Basic plan is for individual access only. Guest passes (2/month) are a Pro plan perk. Upgrade anytime to bring your gym buddy!
